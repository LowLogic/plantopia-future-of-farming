What is Bokashi?

Bokashi is a simple and odorless method to recycle your kitchen waste into a powerful natural fertilizer – right at home.

How It Works

Unlike traditional composting, Bokashi uses fermentation instead of decomposition. This process takes place in a sealed container using beneficial microorganisms. Your food waste is broken down quickly and without unpleasant smells.

Benefits of Bokashi

  • Compact and easy to use indoors
  • No unpleasant odors
  • Suitable for a wide range of food scraps
  • Produces liquid fertilizer (Bokashi tea)
  • Reduces household waste significantly
  • Perfect for sustainable, closed-loop gardening systems

Bokashi & Plantopia

At Plantopia, we believe in circular systems. Bokashi is a perfect match for our smart hydroponic modules. It allows you to recycle your kitchen waste into nutrients that feed your plants. This not only reduces waste but also creates a sustainable nutrient cycle.
